About us!

We are a group of individuals who saw a need for something. We saw students selling textbooks to eachother through facebook and other platforms so we decided to create a site for just that. This takes out the middle man and connects users via email. Users can browse our books currently for sale and select a book they are trying to buy. This opens a new page with a table of all sales entries for that book with different conditions and prices and sellers.
Created by Brandon Cratty, Dean Kromer, Bushan Shah, Eric Brauner, and Chris Ricks.
